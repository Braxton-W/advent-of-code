### Part 1 Task
Count the occurences of "XMAS" in the input. As in a word search, "XMAS" is valid forwards, backwards, vertically, and diagonally.

### Part 2 Task
Count the occurences of "MAS" in the form of an X, all valid forms shown below.
```
M M     M S     S M     S S
 A       A       A       A
S S     M S     S M     M M
```
